Demos

Giveaway-focused commands

The Acclaim bot for Twitch and YouTube has commands built around easily creating giveaways to keep viewers engaged to your stream while you reward them for their support.

Command Management

The built-in command management page in the streamer dashboards allows you to easily change settings of the default commands to control how often people are able to use commands, as well limit who can use each command.

Milestone settings customization

We have created milestones for you and your viewers to celebrate different engagement goals, such as reaching a certain amount of unique chatters and having a number of chat messages sent. The settings page allows you to customize the thresholds for each milestone to fit the goals that you want to reach.

Easy custom command creation

The Acclaim bot allows you to create personalized commands for your viewers to use in your chat to easily give them the info that they may be looking for. This allows you to focus on keeping your stream engaged.

Chat Statistics Dashboard

The built-in streamer dashboard allows you to see how well your stream has been doing in regards of engagement. You can see a top 10 leaderboard for your most active chatters, as well as what commands are mostly used.
